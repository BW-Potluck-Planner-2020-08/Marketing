Changing The Way You Potluck

Bring Harmony To Your Next Potluck

Through extensive research and understanding of the potluck culture, Potluck Planner was created. This all-in-one platform is a proven problem solver for most of the problems encountered in the potluck planning process. Potluck Planner is what potluck enthusiasts have been waiting for!

Your potluck your way. As a host you have full control and flexiblity over your potluck. With the ability to create multiple potlucks, the fun never has to end! By adding menu items you have full control of who brings what. This also avoids the awkwardness of guests bringing the same items or forgetting an item all together. Through our notification system you can remind your guests and insure that no caserole gets left behind.

Just here for the fun? as a guest you are given the ability RSVP to let the host know you will be at the potluck. With our item selector you can select exaclty what you are going to bring. You can cook with peace of mind knowing no one else will bring the same thing. Have a busy schedule? Turn notifications on so you never miss an update of the action.
